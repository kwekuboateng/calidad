About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

Team Calidad is a four member team participating in the 2019 Agri-tech challenge. We seek to use artificial intelligence to detect diseases early enough in poultry farms. Thus Calidad seeks to provide a system application to help farmers take decisions on disease outbreaks on their farms. This smart technology utilizes Artificial Intelligence (A.I) and computer vision to aid farmers to quickly detect anomalies on their farms. Currently, farmers who have large flock sizes have difficulty monitoring the activities of birds. The failure to strictly monitor these activities has led to the loss of several millions of cedis in investment through the death of birds and outbreak quarantines. By using A.I, sensory cameras can be use to capture images and analyze them for abnormalities such infection or unnatural body vitals like high temperatures by using computer algorithms. An alert will then be sent to the famer to take action by either treating or isolating the bird.
